MSc Business Analytics Thesis

AI-Powered Food Recognition Using CNN

This research focused on reducing avoidable food waste in restaurant operations by using image recognition to identify food items, support inventory tracking and improve decision-making around freshness.

  • Built and compared CNN transfer-learning models using ResNet50 and InceptionV3.
  • Framed the technical solution around business value, operational efficiency and sustainability.
  • Used automation logic to support timestamped inventory updates and earlier decision-making.
